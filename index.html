Indraprasth

Indraprasth is committed to providing you a higher quality of life and redefining the standard of living through innovative real estate products. We uniquely differentiate ourselves through our focus on 4 core values: Customer Centricity, Quality, Latest Technology & Transparency.

Our offerings range from residential flats, service apartments, integrated townships to retail shops. We address the most basic needs like Water, Power & Security besides providing creature comforts & high-end conveniences with similar attention that form the important things that drive our business objectives.

The venture by Amar Adeshwar has well-designed infrastructure such as wide internal roads, adequate parking, large open green spaces, effective security systems, besides carefully chosen best quality building materials. Eco-friendly features include waste management, solar systems, and rain water harvesting.
